WE ARE

WΔRP

We are five 14-15 year-old boys from Saint Olave's Grammar School, who are competing in the F1 in Schools competition as a dev class team. We are a team of leaders, innovators, and designers who love STEM based activities, since we believe they provide valuable, real-life experience through hands-on-learning.

ABHINAV MALLADI

Team Manager / Engineer

When I first heard of the F1iS competition, I found it compelling and quickly formed a team. I never knew that it would become such a deep and demanding project at the start, but it is something that I truly enjoy and value. The feeling of winning is something you can't beat, and I want to help my team on its journey.

OUR

JOURNEY

Team Formed - August 2021

WΔRP was formed on August 2021. Originally it contained 5 (different) members - Abhinav M, Aneeq W, Aritro D, Dennis E, and Neal Y. Three of these members are still part of the team to this day.

Members Change - 5th September 2022

Aritro D left the team, and Ashvin A joined the team to fill in his role.

Member Leaves - 21st January 2023

Ashvin A left the team, and no one joined the team to fill in his role. This is because we felt it wouldn't be good to add a new member so soon before regionals.

Entry Class Regionals - 1st March 2023

We competed in the London North regionals. Despite some slow reaction times, we still won fastest car, and also best engineered car. This was a testament to how optimised our entry class was. Thanks to our high quality portfolio and verbal presentation, we also won first place.

Members Change - 6th June 2023

Dennis E left the team. As we would be moving into Dev class next season, we decided to add two new members. They are Michael Q and Thomas H.

First Sponsorship - August 2023

Ansys agreed to become our first sponsor, giving our engineers access to world class CFD software for free. This enabled us to refine our car designs even more than before.

Aosom became a sponsor - October 2023

Aosom became our second sponsor, and first monetary sponsor, funding us for for the foreseeable future. We were, and are extremely grateful to them for their generous donation.

Dev Class Regionals - 10th January 2024

We competed in the London South regionals, and despite facing off against some tough opposition we still came out on top. We also won the Research & Development award for all classes, and were nominated for the Portfolio and Team Identity award. This means that we are going to compete in the UK National Finals later this year!

F1 in Schools

The F1 in Schools challenge is a STEM based, team challenge, targeted at all students between the ages of 9 and 19. The event provides the participating teams with the opportunity to use CAD / CAM software to design, analyse, manafacture, test, and eventually race minituare compressed air powered cars. The cars are raced on a 20m track, and the teams are not only judged by the speed of their car, but also on their portfolios, marketing, verbal presentations, pit display, and more. Learn More...

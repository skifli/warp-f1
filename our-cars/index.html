Our Cars

Accel - 0.154N Drag

Accel is our entry class car. It went through multiple design iterations itself, and there were many more versions before this design, such as 'Sprint' and 'Jump'. In CFD testing there were no severe areas of low pressure, resulting in such a low drag. This design was much better than any design we thought we were capable of, and we were proud of it.

Velocity - 0.115N Drag

Velocity is our development class car. It went through multiple design iterations itself, and there were many more versions before this design, such as 'Andromeda' and 'Bode'. In CFD testing there were no severe high or low pressure regions. This design was much better than any design we thought we were capable of, and we were proud of it.

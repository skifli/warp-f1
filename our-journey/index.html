Our Journey

Team Formed - August 2021

WΔRP was formed on August 2021. Originally it contained 5 (different) members - Abhinav M, Aneeq W, Aritro D, Dennis E, and Neal Y. Three of these members are still part of the team to this day.

Members Change - 5th September 2022

Aritro D leaves the team, and Ashvin A joins the team to fill in his role.

Member Leaves - 21st January 2023

Ashvin A leaves the team, and no one joins the team to fill in his role. This is because we felt it wouldn't be good to add a new member so soon before regionals.

Entry Class Regionals - 1st March 2023

We competed in the London North regionals. Despite some slow reaction times, we still win fastest car, and also best engineered car. This is a testament to how optimised our entry class is. Thanks to our high quality portfolio and verbal presentation, we also win first place.

Members Change - 6th June 2023

Dennis E leaves the team. As we would be moving into Dev class next season, we decide to add two new members. They are Michael Q and Thomas H.

About Us

We are WΔRP

We are a team of 5 boys from Saint Olave's Grammar School. STEM projects are a great way to have fun while expanding your skillset, and F1 in Schools always appealed to us as an amazing way to do so. While we did consider Vex Robotics as well, we all knew that F1 in Schools was our calling.

Our Achievements

From winning entry class, to also getting fastest car and best engineered, our team has never dissapointed, with a plethora of awards to show for our hard and dedicated work. We hope to carry this momentum into Dev class, where we are planning to continue into the UK Nationals, and if all goes to plan, even Worlds. Read More...
